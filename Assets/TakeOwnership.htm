- Right click on your root movies folder and select 'Properties'

- Now click the 'Security' Tab

 

 

- Click 'Edit'

- Click 'Add'

 

- Enter your user name (computer name not needed)

- Click 'Check Names' to fully qualify your username with your computer name

 

 

- Click 'OK'

- Click on your newly added username

- Click the 'Full control - Allow' checkbox

 

 

- Click Apply, then click 'OK' to close that window and go back to the Properties window

- Click 'OK' to close the Properties window

 

If you still get problems, then the permissions haven't been inherited, so...

 

- Right click on your root movies folder and select 'Properties' again

- Click on your newly added username

- Click 'Advanced'

 

Advanced Sec

 

- Click on your newly added username

- Check 'Replace all child object permissions...'

 

 

- Click 'OK'

 

 

- Click 'Yes'

- Click 'OK' to close the Properties window

 

- You may need to repeat this on each on your MC media folders.

 

Job done ;-)
